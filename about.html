Our Journey

Founded in 2010 by coffee enthusiast Raza Aulia, RAZA COFFEE began as a small coffee cart in South Jakarta. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a beloved local spot known for its exceptional brews and warm atmosphere.

Today, with three locations across Jakarta, we continue to uphold our founding principles: quality, sustainability, and community. Our beans are ethically sourced from small farms around the world, and we roast them in-house to ensure the freshest possible flavor in every cup.

At RAZA COFFEE, we believe that great coffee brings people together. That's why we've created spaces where friends can connect, ideas can flourish, and everyone feels welcome.

Our Mission

To enrich our community by providing exceptional coffee experiences while supporting sustainable farming practices and fostering meaningful connections.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Jakarta's premier coffee destination, known for innovation in brewing techniques while staying true to traditional coffee craftsmanship.
