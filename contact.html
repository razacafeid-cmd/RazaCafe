Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer catering services for events?

Yes, we offer catering services for events of all sizes. Our catering menu includes coffee stations, pastries, sandwiches, and desserts. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance for catering inquiries.

Can I reserve a table in advance?

Yes, we accept table reservations for groups of 6 or more. You can make a reservation by calling us directly or using the contact form on this page. For smaller groups, we operate on a first-come, first-served basis.

Do you have vegetarian and vegan options?

Yes, we offer several vegetarian and vegan options. Our menu clearly marks vegetarian (V) and vegan (VG) items. We also offer plant-based milk alternatives including oat, almond, and soy milk for your beverages.

Do you sell coffee beans for home brewing?

Yes, we sell our premium coffee beans in 250g and 500g packages. You can choose from our selection of single-origin beans and blends. Visit our store to purchase beans or contact us for bulk orders.

Is there parking available at your location?

Yes, we have limited parking spaces available in front of our store. There is also additional street parking nearby. For larger groups, we recommend using public transportation or ride-sharing services.
